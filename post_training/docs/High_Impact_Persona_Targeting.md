# High-Impact Persona Targeting Strategy

**Author**: Chin-Yew Lin  
**Date**: November 10, 2025  
**Purpose**: Identify personas who benefit most from each prompt to maximize user value and adoption

---

## Executive Summary

**Core Insight**: Not all personas benefit equally from each prompt. Target the **highest pain ‚Üí highest relief** personas first for maximum impact and advocacy.

**Strategy**: For each prompt, identify personas with:
1. **High Pain**: Problem is acute, frequent, costly
2. **High Receptivity**: Recognize the problem, willing to adopt solutions
3. **High Influence**: Champions who drive broader adoption

**Expected Outcome**: 
- 20-30% of personas drive 70-80% of value
- Focus on "desperate" users ‚Üí Build passionate advocates
- Word-of-mouth from champions accelerates adoption
