## Key Issues Summary

### 1. CAN-02 Meeting Classification Conflation ‚ö†Ô∏è CRITICAL

**Evidence** (mentioned in 4 prompts):
- organizer-1: "lump two types: meeting type and meeting priority"
- organizer-2: "includes both 'meeting type' and 'meeting importance'"
- organizer-3: "includes 'meeting type' and 'meeting importance'"
- schedule-2: "classify 'reschedulable or not reschedulable'"
- collaborate-2: "identify meeting as '1:1' (meeting type)"

**Problem**: CAN-02 is being used for multiple distinct classification tasks:
1. **Meeting Type** (format-based): 1:1, team meeting, customer meeting, all-hands
2. **Meeting Importance** (value-based): important vs. unimportant
3. **Meeting Priority** (ranking-based): high priority vs. low priority
4. **Meeting Reschedulability** (constraint-based): can move vs. cannot move

**Recommendation**: Split into separate tasks:
- **CAN-02A**: Meeting Type Classification (format-based)
- **CAN-02B**: Meeting Importance/Priority Assessment (value-based)
